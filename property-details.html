Overview

Leeds stands as one of the UK’s economic powerhouses, rich in heritage and vibrancy. Nestled west of the picturesque Yorkshire Dales, this city combines a thriving economy with a dynamic urban lifestyle.

Nestled on the outskirts of Shadwell civil parish in north-east Leeds, West Yorkshire, England, this site is situated in the LS17 postcode area. It falls within the Harewood Parish jurisdiction of Leeds City Council and is supported by the Shadwell Parish Council.

This vibrant neighborhood centre is a sought-after destination, known for its robust housing market and coveted status as one of the city’s most desirable areas. Boasting a variety of local shops and amenities, along with a bustling town centre, top-notch schools, and excellent transport connections, it offers residents a convenient and fulfilling lifestyle.

The Site is bordered by Tarn Lane to the north, Brandon Golf Course to the south and southeast, and lush greenery to the east and west. It is primarily surrounded by residential development in the charming area of Shadwell. Additionally, there is a residential dwelling to the northeast of the Site with access from Tarn Lane. Positioned equidistant between the A6120 northern ring road, the A58 Wetherby Road, and the A61 leading to Harewood, the site enjoys an enviable location with convenient access to north Leeds and the neighboring villages.
