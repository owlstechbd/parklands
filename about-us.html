COMMITMENT IS AT THE HEART OF TRAFALGAR’S FAMILY AND ITS GREAT PURPOSE CONTINUES TO UNFOLD.

Leveraging from the Chairman Mr. Kamal Bhatia’s diverse professional background, Trafalgar Properties was established in Dubai in 2013, turning his expertise into an enhanced approach to land investments in the UK. Specializing in offering additional value-added services assignments. Trafalgar distinguished itself by promoting comprehensive and tailored services to match diverse client investment profiles.

OUR HISTORY

With deep- rooted experience in the Project Development field within in the UK and India. Mr. Kamal Bhatia took his first steps towards a journey of unrivaled success over 40 years ago. By establishing a land investments business that could also incorporate an entire master planned community. Mr. Bhatia has created a new approach which would pioneer the urbanization efforts of a nation.

Based on transparent practices in the UK investments market, his flowless reputation has bought innovative and substantial opportunities for the GCC region and international investors. By acquiring an outstanding land bank, Trafalgar has emerged as a leading player in the realtor sector within these regions. Together with his son, Mr. Anurag Bhatia, their initiatives to pursue a value- embracing approach which combines real estate knowledge and modern-day investment challenges have become well known across the UK, UAE, and India.
